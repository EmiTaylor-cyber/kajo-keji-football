Community Impact

How football is changing lives in Kajo Keji County

Building a Stronger Community Through Football

At KKLFA, we believe that football is more than just a game. It's a powerful tool for positive change in our community. Through our various programs and initiatives, we are:

  • 1
    Promoting Youth Development

    Providing structured programs that help young people develop not only as players but as individuals.

  • 2
    Creating Educational Opportunities

    Combining football with education to ensure players have the skills they need for life beyond the pitch.

  • 3
    Building Community Cohesion

    Using football as a platform to bring together different communities and promote peace and understanding.

Our Objectives

The guiding principles behind our work

Develop Football

Promote and develop football at all levels throughout Kajo Keji County, ensuring access for all.

Build Community

Use football as a tool for community development, social cohesion, and positive change.

Nurture Talent

Identify and develop talented players, providing pathways to higher levels of the game.

Educate & Train

Provide education and training for players, coaches, referees, and administrators.

Foster Partnerships

Build partnerships with local and international organizations to strengthen football development.

Promote Health

Use football to promote healthy lifestyles and physical wellbeing in the community.
