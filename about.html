About Us

Learn about the Kajo Keji Local Football Association and our mission

Our Story

The Kajo Keji Local Football Association (KKLFA) was established in 2023 with a vision to develop and promote football in Kajo Keji County, South Sudan. Our association was born out of a passion for the beautiful game and a desire to use football as a tool for positive change in our community.

In the face of numerous challenges, a group of dedicated football enthusiasts came together to create an organization that would not only focus on developing sporting talent but also contribute to the social and economic development of the region.

Since our inception, we have worked tirelessly to create opportunities for young people, establish competitive leagues, and build partnerships with local and international organizations that share our vision.

Today, KKLFA stands as a beacon of hope and a catalyst for change, using the power of football to unite communities, develop talent, and create a brighter future for Kajo Keji County.

Our Mission

To develop and promote football at all levels in Kajo Keji County, creating opportunities for players, coaches, and officials while using the sport as a vehicle for positive social change and community development.

We are committed to establishing sustainable football structures, providing quality training and competition, and ensuring that football is accessible to all regardless of background, gender, or ability.

Our Vision

To be the leading football association in South Sudan, recognized for excellence in football development, community engagement, and organizational governance.

We envision a future where Kajo Keji County is known for its vibrant football culture, where talented players have clear pathways to higher levels of the game, and where football contributes significantly to the social and economic development of our communities.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work

Integrity

We are committed to honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our operations and relationships.

Inclusivity

We believe in creating opportunities for all, regardless of gender, ethnicity, religion, or socioeconomic background.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do, from player development to organizational management.

We are committed to using football as a tool for community development and social cohesion.

Innovation

We embrace creative approaches and new ideas to address challenges and create opportunities.

Sustainability

We are focused on building sustainable structures and programs that will benefit generations to come.
