Fan Stories

Hear from passionate KKLFA supporters

Emmy Wani

Kajo Keji Stars supporter for 5 years

I've been supporting Kajo Keji Stars since they were formed. The community that has built up around the team is incredible - we're like one big family. Match days are the highlight of my week, and I've made lifelong friends through our shared passion for the team.

John travels to every home and away match, and has only missed three games in five years. He is one of the founding members of the Kajo Keji Stars Supporters club and helps organize transport for away matches.

Sarah Kenyi

Kajo Keji United supporter for 3 years

Football has always been a passion of mine, but I never had a local team to support until KKLFA was formed. Now I'm at every United match with my family, and my children are growing up with the same love for the game and our community team.

Sarah leads the United Faithful's community outreach program, organizing charity events and using football as a way to bring people together. She has also started a women's supporters group to encourage more female fans to attend matches.

David Ladu

KKLFA supporter since 2019

What KKLFA has done for our community goes beyond football. It's given us something positive to rally around, a sense of identity and pride. I support all the teams in the association because I believe in what they're building here in Kajo Keji County.

David is the founder of the Kajo Keji Football Collective, which supports all teams in the association. He produces a weekly podcast about KKLFA and has documented the growth of football in the county through photography and writing.

Match Day Experience

Everything you need to know for your visit to KKLFA matches

Before the Match

  • Tickets

    Purchase tickets online or at the stadium on match day. Arrive early to avoid queues.

  • Getting to the Stadium

    Kajo Keji Stadium is located in the center of town. Limited parking is available, so public transport or walking is recommended.

  • Pre-Match Food & Drink

    Several local restaurants and cafes around the stadium offer special match day menus. The Fan Zone opens 2 hours before kick-off.

During the Match

  • Supporter Sections

    Each team has a dedicated supporter section. Check your ticket for your designated area.

  • Chants & Songs

    Join in with the fan clubs' chants and songs. Don't worry if you don't know them - you'll pick them up quickly!

  • Match Programs

    Purchase a match program for 200 SSP to get team news, player profiles, and more.

Match Day Tips

  • Arrive at least 30 minutes before kick-off to find your seat and soak up the atmosphere
  • Wear team colors to show your support
  • Bring cash for food, drinks, and merchandise
  • Check the weather forecast and dress appropriately
  • Follow stadium announcements and steward instructions
