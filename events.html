Host an Event with KKLFA

Are you interested in organizing a football event in Kajo Keji County? KKLFA can help you plan and execute successful football events, from friendly matches to tournaments and community activities.

We can provide:

  • Technical expertise and guidance
  • Access to facilities and equipment
  • Qualified referees and officials
  • Promotion and publicity
  • Volunteer support

Whether you're a school, community organization, business, or individual, we're here to support football initiatives that align with our mission of developing football and transforming communities in Kajo Keji County.
