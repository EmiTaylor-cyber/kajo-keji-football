Ticket Information

Support your favorite team by attending matches at Kajo Keji Stadium and other venues across the county. Tickets for all KKLFA matches can be purchased online or at the stadium on match days.

Ticket Prices:

  • Premier League Matches: 500 SSP (Regular), 1,000 SSP (VIP)
  • Kajo Keji Cup Matches: 700 SSP (Regular), 1,500 SSP (VIP)
  • Women's League Matches: 300 SSP (Regular), 700 SSP (VIP)
  • Youth Competitions: 200 SSP (Regular)

Season Tickets:

Season tickets for the 2025 season are available, giving you access to all home matches for your chosen team. Contact the KKLFA office for more information.
