Our Achievements

Celebrating the success and milestones of Kajo Keji Local Football Association

Trophy Cabinet

Major trophies and awards won by KKLFA teams

2024

Central Equatoria Cup

Kajo Keji Stars

Kajo Keji Stars defeated Juba City FC 2-1 in the final to claim the prestigious Central Equatoria Cup for the first time in the club's history.

March 2024 Juba Stadium
2023

Kajo Keji Cup

Kajo Keji United

Kajo Keji United won the inaugural Kajo Keji Cup, defeating local rivals Kajo Keji Stars in a thrilling penalty shootout after a 2-2 draw in regular time.

December 2023 Kajo Keji Stadium
2023

South Sudan Youth Championship (U-17)

KKLFA Youth Academy

The KKLFA Youth Academy team won the South Sudan Youth Championship, showcasing the success of the association's youth development program.

August 2023 Wau Stadium
2023

Kajo Keji Community Shield

Kajo Keji Stars

Kajo Keji Stars won the first-ever Kajo Keji Community Shield, defeating Kansuk FC 3-0 in the season-opening match.

May 2023 Kajo Keji Stadium
