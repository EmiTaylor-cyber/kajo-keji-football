Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our newsletter

How often will I receive the newsletter?

You can choose your preferred frequency when signing up. We offer weekly digests, bi-weekly updates, monthly roundups, or immediate updates as events happen. You can change your preference at any time by updating your subscription settings.

How do I unsubscribe if I no longer want to receive the newsletter?

Unsubscribing is easy. Every newsletter includes an "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom. Simply click this link and follow the instructions to stop receiving emails. You can also contact us directly at newsletter@kklfa.org to request removal from our mailing list.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your personal information is stored securely and will never be sold or shared with third parties without your consent. We use your information solely for the purpose of sending you the newsletter and providing the services you've requested. You can review our full privacy policy here.

Can I forward the newsletter to friends and family?

Absolutely! We encourage you to share our newsletter with anyone who might be interested in KKLFA news and events. Each newsletter also includes a "Forward to a Friend" option. However, we recommend that regular readers subscribe directly to ensure they don't miss any issues and can manage their own preferences.

Will I receive special offers through the newsletter?

Yes, newsletter subscribers often receive exclusive offers, discounts on merchandise, early access to ticket sales, and invitations to special events. You can customize your interests when signing up to ensure you receive the types of offers most relevant to you.

Can I contribute content to the newsletter?

We welcome content contributions from our community! If you have match reports, photos, stories, or other content you'd like to share, please email them to content@kklfa.org. Our editorial team reviews all submissions and may include them in future newsletters with appropriate credit.
