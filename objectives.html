Our Objectives

The guiding principles and goals that drive our work at Kajo Keji Local Football Association

Our Mission

What drives us every day

The Kajo Keji Local Football Association (KKLFA) exists to develop and promote football at all levels in Kajo Keji County, creating opportunities for players, coaches, and officials while using the sport as a vehicle for positive social change and community development.

We are committed to establishing sustainable football structures, providing quality training and competition, and ensuring that football is accessible to all regardless of background, gender, or ability.

Through our work, we aim to harness the power of football to unite communities, develop talent, and create a brighter future for Kajo Keji County.

Detailed Objectives

A closer look at our specific goals

Football Development

Establish Structured Leagues and Competitions

Organize and manage regular league competitions for different age groups and levels, ensuring consistent playing opportunities for all participants.

Develop Youth Football Programs

Create comprehensive youth development programs that focus on both football skills and personal development, providing a safe and supportive environment for young players to grow.

Promote Women's Football

Actively promote and develop women's football through dedicated programs, competitions, and initiatives that address the specific challenges and opportunities in women's football.

Improve Football Facilities and Infrastructure

Work towards improving football facilities and infrastructure in Kajo Keji County, including playing fields, training facilities, and equipment, to provide better conditions for players and teams.

Community Building

Organize Community Football Events

Host regular community football events that bring people together, celebrate local culture, and create a sense of belonging and pride in Kajo Keji County.

Implement Social Impact Programs

Develop and implement programs that use football as a tool to address social issues, such as education, health, gender equality, and social inclusion.

Promote Peace and Unity

Use football as a platform to promote peace, understanding, and unity among different communities in Kajo Keji County, fostering dialogue and cooperation.

Support Vulnerable Groups

Create targeted initiatives to support vulnerable groups in the community, including children, youth at risk, people with disabilities, and those affected by conflict or displacement.

Talent Development

Establish Talent Identification Programs

Develop systematic approaches to identify talented players across Kajo Keji County, ensuring that potential is recognized regardless of location or background.

Create Player Development Pathways

Establish clear pathways for player development, from grassroots to elite levels, with appropriate support and guidance at each stage.

Provide Specialized Training

Offer specialized training programs for talented players, focusing on technical, tactical, physical, and mental aspects of the game to help them reach their full potential.

Facilitate Progression Opportunities

Create connections and partnerships that provide opportunities for talented players to progress to higher levels of the game, including regional and national teams, academies, and clubs.

Education & Training

Organize Coaching Courses

Provide regular coaching education opportunities, from introductory to advanced levels, to develop a skilled and knowledgeable coaching workforce in Kajo Keji County.

Train and Develop Referees

Implement referee development programs to ensure high-quality officiating in all competitions, including training, mentoring, and assessment.

Provide Administrative Training

Offer training and support for club administrators and officials to enhance organizational capacity and governance within local football.

Combine Football with Education

Develop programs that combine football with educational opportunities for players, emphasizing the importance of education alongside sporting development.

Partnerships

Develop Relationships with Football Governing Bodies

Build strong relationships with national and regional football governing bodies to ensure alignment with broader football development strategies and access to resources and support.

Partner with NGOs and Community Organizations

Collaborate with non-governmental organizations and community groups to leverage resources, expertise, and networks for greater impact in football and community development.

Secure Sponsorships and Funding

Develop partnerships with businesses, donors, and funding agencies to secure the financial resources needed to implement programs and initiatives effectively.

Collaborate with Educational Institutions

Work with schools, colleges, and universities to promote football, provide educational opportunities for players, and develop research and knowledge in football development.

Health Promotion

Organize Health Awareness Campaigns

Use football as a platform to raise awareness about important health issues affecting the community, such as malaria prevention, HIV/AIDS, nutrition, and mental health.

Promote Physical Activity

Encourage regular physical activity through football programs and initiatives, contributing to improved physical health and wellbeing in the community.

Address Community Health Issues

Develop targeted programs that address specific health challenges in Kajo Keji County, working with health professionals and organizations to maximize impact.

Provide Health Education

Integrate health education into football programs, ensuring that players and community members have access to accurate information about health and wellbeing.

Implementation Strategy

How we plan to achieve our objectives

Short-Term Goals (1-2 Years)

  • Establish a structured local league with at least 8 participating teams
  • Develop a youth football program reaching at least 200 young players
  • Train at least 20 coaches through certified coaching courses
  • Organize at least 4 community football events per year
  • Establish partnerships with at least 3 local organizations
  • Develop a women's football program with at least 2 teams

Medium-Term Goals (3-4 Years)

  • Expand the local league to include multiple divisions and age groups
  • Develop a comprehensive talent identification and development program
  • Establish a referee development program with at least 15 trained referees
  • Implement at least 3 social impact programs using football as a tool
  • Secure sustainable funding through diverse sources
  • Improve at least 2 football facilities in Kajo Keji County

Long-Term Goals (5+ Years)

  • Establish Kajo Keji County as a hub for football development in South Sudan
  • Have players from KKLFA programs representing national teams
  • Develop a sustainable football ecosystem with strong clubs, competitions, and development programs
  • Create measurable social impact through football in areas such as education, health, and community cohesion
  • Establish a dedicated football facility with training grounds, administrative offices, and community spaces
  • Become a model for community-based football associations in the region

Monitoring and Evaluation

How we measure our progress and impact

Key Performance Indicators

We track our progress through a range of quantitative and qualitative indicators:

Participation

  • Number of registered players (total, by age group, by gender)
  • Number of teams and clubs
  • Number of matches and tournaments organized
  • Attendance at events and activities

Development

  • Number of trained coaches, referees, and administrators
  • Number of players progressing to higher levels
  • Quality of play and competition (assessed through technical evaluations)
  • Improvement in facilities and infrastructure

Impact Assessment

We assess our impact through various methods:

Community Impact

  • Surveys and feedback from community members
  • Case studies of individual and community transformation
  • Assessment of social indicators in target communities
  • Testimonials and stories of change

Organizational Development

  • Financial sustainability and resource mobilization
  • Governance and management effectiveness
  • Quality and reach of partnerships
  • Recognition and reputation within the community and beyond

Reporting and Learning

We are committed to transparency, accountability, and continuous learning. We regularly:

  • Produce annual reports on our activities, achievements, and challenges
  • Hold review meetings with stakeholders to share progress and gather feedback
  • Document lessons learned and best practices
  • Adjust our strategies and approaches based on evaluation findings
