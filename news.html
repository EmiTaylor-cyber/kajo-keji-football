Kajo Keji Stars Triumph in Thrilling Local Derby

In a match that will be remembered for years to come, Kajo Keji Stars secured a dramatic 3-2 victory over their local rivals Kajo Keji United in front of a record crowd at Kajo Keji Stadium.

The match, which was part of the Kajo Keji Cup quarter-finals, saw Stars come from behind twice before scoring a winner in the 89th minute through captain Victor Ladu.
