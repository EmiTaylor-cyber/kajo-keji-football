Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about donating to KKLFA

How is my donation used?

Your donation directly supports our programs in youth development, facility improvement, coach education, equipment provision, and community outreach. We allocate funds based on current needs, with approximately:

  • 40% to youth development programs
  • 25% to facility development and maintenance
  • 15% to equipment and supplies
  • 10% to coach and referee education
  • 10% to administrative costs

We provide annual reports to all donors detailing how funds have been used.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

KKLFA is registered as a non-profit sports organization in South Sudan. For South Sudanese taxpayers, donations are tax-deductible according to current regulations. For international donors, tax deductibility depends on your country's laws regarding foreign charitable donations. We recommend consulting with a tax professional for specific advice.

Can I specify how my donation is used?

Yes, you can designate your donation for specific programs or initiatives. When making your donation, simply indicate your preference in the notes section or select one of our specific donation options like player sponsorship. If you have a particular area of interest not listed, please contact us directly to discuss your preferences.

How do I donate equipment?

You can donate equipment by selecting the "Donate Equipment" option on this page and filling out the form. Alternatively, you can contact us directly to arrange equipment donations. We accept new and gently used football equipment including balls, boots, kits, training gear, and first aid supplies. We can arrange collection for large donations or provide our address for shipping or drop-off.

Can I volunteer instead of donating money?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers in many capacities, including coaching, event organization, administration, and more. Your time and skills are valuable contributions to our mission. Please visit our Join the Team page for information about volunteer opportunities, or contact us directly to discuss how you can help.

How can I verify that KKLFA is a legitimate organization?

KKLFA is officially registered with the South Sudan Ministry of Youth and Sports and the South Sudan Football Association. We maintain transparent financial records and publish annual reports. You can verify our status by contacting these organizations directly. We also encourage potential donors to visit our facilities and meet our team in person. For any verification concerns, please contact our administrative office.
