  • Recruit, train, and develop referees at all levels
  • Assign referees to matches and competitions
  • Evaluate referee performance and provide feedback
  • Organize referee education programs and workshops

Governance Structure

The KKLFA operates with a clear governance structure to ensure effective management and accountability:

  1. General Assembly - The highest decision-making body, consisting of all member clubs and stakeholders, meets annually.
  2. Executive Committee - Elected by the General Assembly, responsible for overall leadership and management.
  3. Standing Committees - Specialized committees focusing on specific areas of operation.
  4. Secretariat - Day-to-day administration and implementation of programs and activities.

This structure ensures that KKLFA operates with transparency, inclusivity, and effectiveness in achieving its mission and objectives.
