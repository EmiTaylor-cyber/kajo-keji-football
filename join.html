How You Can Join

There are many ways to be part of the KKLFA family

Join as a Player

We welcome players of all ages and skill levels to join our various teams and development programs. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting out, there's a place for you at KKLFA.

Requirements:

  • Commitment to regular training and matches
  • Positive attitude and team spirit
  • Willingness to learn and improve
  • Respect for coaches, officials, and fellow players

Available Programs:

  • Youth Development Program (ages 8-16)
  • Senior Men's Team (ages 17+)
  • Women's Team (ages 16+)
  • Veterans Team (ages 35+)

Join as a Coach

We're always looking for passionate and dedicated coaches to help develop our players and teams. Whether you're an experienced coach or looking to start your coaching journey, we have opportunities for you.

Requirements:

  • Knowledge of football fundamentals
  • Ability to communicate effectively with players
  • Commitment to player development both on and off the field
  • Willingness to participate in coaching education programs

Available Positions:

  • Head Coaches
  • Assistant Coaches
  • Goalkeeper Coaches
  • Youth Development Coaches

Join as a Referee

Referees play a crucial role in the game, ensuring fair play and maintaining the integrity of the sport. We provide training and development opportunities for referees at all levels.

Requirements:

  • Good knowledge of the Laws of the Game
  • Physical fitness
  • Strong decision-making skills
  • Ability to remain calm under pressure

Benefits:

  • Referee training and certification
  • Regular officiating opportunities
  • Mentoring from experienced referees
  • Pathway to higher levels of officiating

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about joining KKLFA

What are the membership fees?

Membership fees vary depending on the role and program. Players in our youth development program pay a nominal fee to cover basic costs, while senior team players have a slightly higher fee. Volunteer positions do not require a membership fee. Specific fee information will be provided during the application process.

Do I need previous experience to join?

No, previous experience is not always required. We welcome individuals at all skill and experience levels. For players, we have development programs suitable for beginners. For coaches and referees, we provide training and mentorship. What's most important is your enthusiasm, commitment, and willingness to learn.

How much time commitment is required?

Time commitments vary by role. Players typically have 2-3 training sessions per week plus matches. Coaches are expected to attend all team training sessions and matches. Referees are scheduled based on availability. Volunteer commitments can be flexible, depending on the role and your availability. We understand that people have other commitments and try to be as accommodating as possible.

What is the application process?

The application process begins with submitting the form on this page. After review, we'll contact you to schedule an interview or tryout, depending on the role you're applying for. For players, this may involve a skills assessment. For coaches and referees, we'll discuss your experience and qualifications. The final step is orientation and onboarding to welcome you to the KKLFA family.

Is there an age limit for joining?

Our youth development program accepts players from age 8 and up. Senior teams are generally for ages 17 and above. For coaching and refereeing positions, applicants should be at least 16 years old, though exceptions may be made. Volunteer opportunities are available for all ages, with appropriate roles assigned based on age and capabilities.
